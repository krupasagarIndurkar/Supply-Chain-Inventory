# Supply-Chain-Inventory
1. Inventory Levels
Some products have excess stock
Some products are frequently out of stock

2. Stock Turnover
Fast-moving items sell quickly
Slow-moving items stay longer in warehouse

3. Supplier Performance
Some suppliers deliver on time
Others have frequent delays.

4. Order Fulfillment Rate
Not all orders are fulfilled on time
Backorders exist for certain products.

6. Backorder Analysis
Backorders increase when stock is unavailable
Specific products contribute more

7. Delivery Performance
On-time delivery % varies
Delays mostly during high demand

8. Warehouse Efficiency
Some warehouses perform better
Others show delays

9. Cost Analysis
Holding cost increases with excess inventory
Stockouts lead to revenue loss

👉 Insight:
1. Poor inventory balance → leads to overstocking & stockouts
2. Supplier reliability directly impacts operations
3. Inventory shortage affects customer satisfaction
4. Needs stock for high-demand products
5. Logistics needs improvement during peak periods
6. Operations are not equally optimized
7. Balance between cost & availability is critical
